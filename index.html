Our Story

Ward was born from the entrepreneurial spirit of three close friends who shared not only a strong friendship, but also similar values and a desire to build something of their own.

We’ve always been drawn to clean, functional design—products that are practical without sacrificing style. When we came across minimalist wallets with built-in cardholders, we were instantly hooked. They felt different: compact, sleek, and efficient—exactly the kind of product we’d want to use ourselves.

Realizing there was an opportunity to bring that concept to more people, we decided to create our own version—one that reflected our standards for quality and detail.

That’s how Ward came to life.
